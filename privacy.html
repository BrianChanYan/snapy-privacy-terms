Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Snapy mobile application ("Snapy", "we", "our", or "us"). It explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our app and related services.

Our Commitment

Snapy is committed to handling your information in a responsible and transparent manner.

We only request the following permissions to the extent necessary to provide core application functionality:

  • Camera (for taking photos)
  • Photo Library/Album (for saving your work)
  • Notifications (for reminders or push features)

Snapy will never enable or use the above permissions without your explicit consent.

Data Processing and Storage

1. All image processing and storage is performed entirely on your device.

2. Snapy does not upload, store, or share any photos, videos, or image-related data to servers or cloud storage.

3. Your creative content exists only on your device. We cannot access or view it.

Login and Account Information

Snapy provides the following login options:

  • Apple Sign-In
  • Google Sign-In
  • Guest Mode

You can enjoy all major features even when using Guest Mode.

If you choose to log in with an account, we will only collect:

  • Basic account identification information (such as email and username)
  • In-app settings (for synchronization across multiple devices)

This data is used solely for authentication and account management purposes.

Snapy will not, without your consent:

  • Extract other personal information;
  • Disclose data to third-party software;
  • Provide data to advertisers.

Third-Party Services

Snapy uses Google Firebase to provide the following services:

  • Push notifications
  • User authentication
  • Application settings synchronization

Firebase serves solely as a technical service provider.

Snapy does not upload or store user image data through Firebase.

For more information, please refer to the following Firebase privacy documentation:

  • Firebase Privacy and Security Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics Data Collection
  • Firebase Analytics Data Collection

Policy Updates

Snapy commits to:

  • Clearly inform you in advance if future updates involve new data collection or sharing practices;
  • Request your explicit consent again before enabling any new features;
  • Provide complete explanations within the app for major updates and prompt you to review them.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions regarding Snapy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us through "Settings → Help and Support → Contact Us" within the application.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of November 1, 2025.
